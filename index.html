The cornerstone of Ladd's Addition.

Walk through the doors and you feel it. Teachers who stay for decades. Parents who know each other by name. Kids who actually want to be here.

Art, music, recess in the rain. The kind of school people move to the neighborhood for. Abernethy has been the heart of this community since 1925.
